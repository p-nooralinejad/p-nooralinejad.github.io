Notable Project

My undergrad thesis. The project is advised by Dr. Hesham Faili and I'm cooperating with Afshin Hosseinali pour. In this project, I have to program an algorithm which takes a thesis, abstract, or any essay and assign some hierarchical topics.
In this project, my partner (Kosar Pourahmadi) and I, designed a robot which uses ultrasonic sensors to detect obstacles and according to speed and direction, it used its processing unit to avoid collision. We used AVR microcontroller as the processing unit and mount the designed chip on a remote control car.
As final project of AI course, the tutor assigned a project to us in which we should have designed a 3-layered neural network with training to train MNIST dataset. Instead, my partner (Kosar Pourahmadi) and I, designed a fully operational dynamic-layered framework which had the capability to train dense, convolutional, pooling, and recurrent layers. It had primitive activation functions like sigmoid, softmax, and relu. We also implemented cross entropy and mean squared error loss function.
Done in my high school, my friends and I implemented a 3D pingpong game and an intelligent agent for it. The agent was designed using two simutanous Reinforcement Learning algorithm. One for prediction of location where the ball hit the table, and the other for the proper reaction according to the location, speed, and direction of the ball.
Our team in high school decided to design a kinect-like device for our pingpong game to simplify gameplay. We used reinforcement learning to predict location of junctions and keypoints. However, the alogrithm was only able to predict elbows, head, neck, shoulders, and wrists.
